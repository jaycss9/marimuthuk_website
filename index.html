About Me

Dr. Marimuthu Karuppiah is currently working as an Associate Professor in the School of Computing Science and Engineering (SCOPE) at SRM University, Vellore.

Prior to joining SRM University, he held the academic position of Senior Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Syed Ammal Engineering College, Ramanathapuram, India from March 2005 to November 2008.
